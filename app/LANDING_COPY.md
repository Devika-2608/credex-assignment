## FAQ (5 real Q&As)

### Q1: Is this really free?

**A:** Yes — the audit is completely free. We only make money if you decide to buy discounted AI credits through Credex after seeing your savings. No obligation, no credit card required.

### Q2: How accurate is the audit?

**A:** Every price comes directly from official vendor pricing pages (Cursor, GitHub, Anthropic, OpenAI, etc.). We cite every source in PRICING_DATA.md. The math is hardcoded and deterministic — no AI hallucinations on your savings numbers.

### Q3: My company uses enterprise plans with custom pricing. Does this still work?

**A:** The audit works best for standard plans (Pro, Team, Business). For enterprise plans, we'll show you the public alternative — but your best savings may come from talking to Credex directly about discounted enterprise credits.

### Q4: Do you store my data? Is it private?

**A:** We store your email and audit results only if you choose to save the report. Shareable URLs strip all identifying info (no email, no company name). You can delete your data anytime by emailing us.

### Q5: What tools do you support?

**A:** Currently: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, and Gemini. We're adding more based on user requests — tell us what you need and we'll prioritize it.
