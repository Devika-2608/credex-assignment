# Economics

## What's a converted lead worth to Credex?

**Assumptions:**
- Average AI tool spend for a startup: $500-2,000/month
- Credex offers 30-50% discounts on credits
- Credex margin: ~15-20% of transaction value

**Calculation:**
- Average customer spends $1,000/month on AI tools
- Credex saves them 40% → $400/month savings
- Credex charges 20% margin → $80/month revenue per customer
- Annual revenue per customer: $80 × 12 = $960
- Customer lifetime (conservative): 18 months → $1,440 LTV

**Lead value:** A lead that converts is worth ~$1,400 LTV

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by channel

| Channel | CAC | Why |
|---------|-----|-----|
| Organic (Hacker News, Reddit) | $0 | Free if post goes viral |
| Twitter/X (viral share) | $0 | Users share their audit results |
| LinkedIn (founder posts) | $0 | Organic reach |
| Discord/Slack communities | $0 | Post in AI/builders groups |
| Google Ads (later stage) | $50-100 | Only after validation |

My GTM focuses on $0 channels first.

## Conversion funnel estimates

| Stage | Conversion | Notes |
|-------|------------|-------|
| Landing → Audit started | 40% | Headline is clear |
| Audit started → Results viewed | 80% | Form is simple |
| Results → Email capture | 25% | We show value first |
| Email → Credex consultation | 10% | Only high-savings cases |
| Consultation → Credit purchase | 30% | Sales conversation |

**Overall conversion:** 40% × 80% × 25% × 10% × 30% = **0.24%**

**CAC math:** For every 10,000 visitors → 24 customers
- Customer LTV: $1,440
- Total revenue: 24 × $1,440 = $34,560
- At $0 CAC, this is highly profitable

## What would have to be true for $1M ARR in 18 months?

**Target:** $1,000,000 ARR ÷ $960 ARPU = ~1,042 customers

**Traffic needed:** 1,042 customers ÷ 0.24% conversion = ~434,000 visitors

**Per month:** 434,000 ÷ 18 months = 24,000 visitors/month

**Is this realistic?** Yes — a single viral Hacker News post can drive 50k+ visitors in a day. If just 10% of visitors share their audit (viral loop), growth compounds.

**Risks:**
- Conversion rates might be lower in reality
- Need to maintain pricing accuracy (vendor prices change)
- Competitors could build similar tool

**Mitigations:**
- Add more tools monthly
- Build email nurturing sequence
- Partner with AI influencers
